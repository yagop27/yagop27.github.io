About Me

Hello, I’m Yago Pais, an aspiring Python developer with a solid background in Physics. After graduating in Brazil, I dedicated several years to teaching, where I found great fulfillment in helping students understand and appreciate the intricacies of science.

As I transition into the tech industry, I am eager to take on new challenges and apply my analytical skills to programming. My experience in education has equipped me with a strong problem-solving mindset and the ability to communicate complex ideas clearly.

I am committed to continuous learning and growth in programming, particularly in Python, and I am excited about the opportunities that lie ahead. Thank you for visiting my website—I look forward to connecting with others who share my passion for technology!
